How it works?

While the Unit is Plug and Play in nature, you need to observe the following few simple steps to ensure trouble free operation: -

  • Take out the Main Unit from the package carefully. Be cautious not to damage the UV-C Lamps, which are pre-fitted for the ease of operation.
  • Assemble the stand as per the diagram shown & Fix the Unit with the fixing screws.
  • When fixing the Unit with the stand, ensure matching of the marking on both, left &right end, for ease of bolting /screwing from bottom end.
  • Place the Unit at the centre of the room and connect the Power cord to Main Switch. Recommended to Connect via Extension Board to a suitable Main Power Supply(5Amp- AC Main Socket).
  • Vacate the room & switch ON the unit.
  • Keep the Unit switched ON for around 25- 30 mins (Ideal for covering100 Sqft to 150 Sqft carpet area).
  • Ensure that Power connection is switched OFF before entering the room.
  • Move the unit to another room and follow the above-mentioned steps to ensure a sterilized & healthy environment for you and your beloved ones.
